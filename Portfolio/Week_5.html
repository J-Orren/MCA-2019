Video Game Score Composition Collection

Basic Metadata

In this week's task, I focussed on creating a schema to describe the music metadata found within my collection/dataset.

For my personal collection, I believed the following types of data would be most important for cross-referencing and analysing scores from different video games:


Title - the title of the track.

Game - the title of the game itself from which the music was composed for.

Composer - the original composer for the score itself.

Year of Composition - the year when the game/composition was released.

Draft - the draft number of transcribed piece of music.

Draft Date - when the current draft was transcribed.

Revised by - the person responsible for transcribing the current draft.

Game Genre - the type of game the music comes from (a survival horror game would most likely have a vastly different score to a first-person shooter for example).


Once I had created my schema, I implemented it into my original MEI file.
